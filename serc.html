MiniMEE — the flagship

// Product · Pre-launch

An AI-powered PCB design assistant.

You describe what you want to build — a motor driver, a sensor breakout, a simple audio filter. MiniMEE generates the schematic, suggests the components, flags design errors as you go, and exports something you can actually send to a fab house. It's what KiCad could have been if it had started with a chatbot interface and an LLM.

Our beachhead is the 15M+ engineering students worldwide. Our ambition is every hardware engineer who's ever opened a schematic editor and felt overwhelmed.

The Robot Arm — hardware layer

// Hardware · In build

4-axis pick-and-place robotic system.

Designed for sub-millimeter placement accuracy. NEMA 17 and NEMA 8 stepper motors, TMC2209 silent drivers, ESP32 control, vision-assisted alignment via an onboard JEPA model, 24V-powered vacuum end effector on a hollow-shaft wrist. Firmware communicates over USB serial to a Flask backend that drives the web chat interface.

Filed provisional patent with USPTO on the AI-Assisted Robotic Arm System for Automated Component Assembly, Education, and Remote Operations.
